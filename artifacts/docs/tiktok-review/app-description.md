# App Description for TikTok App Review

**App Name:** OCTOPUS AI LAB
**Category:** Productivity / Video Publishing

## Short Description
OCTOPUS AI LAB is an intelligent video publishing suite that helps creators manage and distribute their vertical video content across social networks efficiently, utilizing official APIs for seamless drafting and direct posting.

## Long Description
OCTOPUS AI LAB empowers video creators to streamline their content distribution. Once a creator approves a video in our dashboard, our TikTok Bridge uses the official TikTok Login Kit and Content Posting APIs to push the video directly to the user's TikTok account. We prioritize user privacy and explicitly request user consent prior to initiating any publishing actions. Our platform acts as a bridge between the creator's local studio and their official TikTok audience, ensuring all content remains fully compliant with TikTok's community guidelines and platform rules.
