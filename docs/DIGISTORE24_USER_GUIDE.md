# Digistore24 User Guide

This guide explains how to use the Digistore24 integration in OCTOPUS OS as a user to find and promote affiliate products.

## Overview

OCTOPUS OS allows you to connect your Digistore24 account securely, import products manually, generate optimized tracking links, and instantly build high-converting AI-driven campaigns.

## Finding Products to Promote

Digistore24 does not provide a programmatic "Marketplace Search" API to browse public offers securely. Therefore, finding a product requires you to browse the official Digistore24 Marketplace directly on their website:

1. Go to the [Digistore24 Marketplace](https://www.digistore24.com/en/home/marketplace).
2. Browse or search for a product you want to promote.
3. Click the **Promote Now** button next to the product.
   - If the vendor automatically approves affiliates, you will instantly receive your Promolink.
   - If the vendor requires manual approval, you must wait for their approval before your Promolink is active.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> A syntactically valid Promolink does not guarantee that you will earn a commission. The vendor must have approved your partnership in Digistore24. OCTOPUS OS tracks this under "Partnership Status".

## Importing a Product to OCTOPUS OS

Once you have your product details or Promolink, you can import it into OCTOPUS OS.

1. In OCTOPUS OS, navigate to **Affiliate Networks**.
2. Click on the **Manual Import** tab.
3. Select **Digistore24** from the Network dropdown.
4. Enter the **Product Name**.
5. (Optional but Recommended) Enter the **Product ID**.
6. (Optional) Paste the **Promolink** provided by Digistore24.
7. Click **Import Product**.

The product will now appear in your **Marketplace** tab inside OCTOPUS OS.

## Promoting with AI

Once a product is imported:

1. Go to the **Marketplace** tab in OCTOPUS OS.
2. Find your product and click **⚡ Promote with AI**.
3. OCTOPUS OS will securely generate a unique Tracking Link (with a special `campaignKey` to track attributions specifically for this campaign).
4. You will automatically be redirected to the **Campaigns** page.
5. The Campaign Creator will open, pre-filled with the Product Name and your unique Tracking Link.
6. Select your target platforms and AI agents, then click **Create Campaign**.

## Viewing Your Tracking Links

All generated tracking links are securely stored in the system. They are generated identically every time you try to promote the exact same product for the same campaign, ensuring you don't end up with thousands of redundant tracking links in your Digistore24 reporting.
